about me

Hello! I am Rayhan a passionate and independent developer on a mission to build something extraordinary. I don't just write code; I create experiences.

What I Do: I am deeply immersed in the world of programming, with expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Kotlin, and C#. Whether it's crafting responsive websites or developing functional apps and games, I love solving complex problems through technology.

3D & Creativity: Beyond the screen of code, I am a 3D Artist. I use Blender to bring imagination to life, creating 3D designs and funny, engaging animations. For me, 3D design is not just a skill—it’s a way to tell stories that make people smile.

My Vision: My goal is to become a fully independent programmer. This website is my digital home where I share my journey, my games, my apps, and my creative contributions. Soon, I will be publishing my exclusive 3D designs here as well.

Beyond the Keyboard: When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll find me capturing the world through my lens or exploring new horizons. I am an avid photographer and a traveler at heart. Traveling gives me the fresh perspective I need to keep my creativity flowing. Feel free to explore my work and stay tuned for more updates!
